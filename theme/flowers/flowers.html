Gardening seems to be too much work for me, but my husband enjoys it. He has planted many flowers in our yard mainly for bees. I found interesting the green flower called Helleborus orientalis in the first row. They were green before blooming. I enjoy taking picture of them in spring. Some flowers attracted many bumble bees especially spanish lavender which is third one from the left. There are other kinds of lavender as well. We also have other flowers like Bee balm and Salvia for Humming birds.
