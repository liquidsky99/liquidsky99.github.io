My husband has planted many flowers in our yard mainly to attract bees. I found interesting the green flower called Helleborus orientalis in the first row. They were green before blooming. I enjoy taking picture of them in spring. Some flowers attracted many bumble bees especially spanish lavender which is third one from the left. We also have other flowers like Bee balm and Salvia for Humming birds.
